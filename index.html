Profile

Research shaped by hardware, control, and real power systems.

Dr. Raveendhra has worked across academia, industrial R&D, startups, and international research centers, including IIT Roorkee, NC State University's FREEDM Systems Center, American University of Sharjah, ALTEN GmbH Germany, and Indian engineering institutions.

His work spans converter design, dynamic modelling, controller implementation, electric vehicle power electronics, solar PV conversion, hybrid energy storage systems, FPGA and DSP-based control, and laboratory validation of power hardware.

Power conversion for cleaner, smarter energy systems.
